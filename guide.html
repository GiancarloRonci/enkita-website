User Guide

Everything you need to know to get the most out of Enkita.

Welcome to Enkita

Study English vocabulary with flashcards and the spaced repetition system.

App Philosophy

  • Enkita is designed for Italian speakers who want to learn or consolidate English starting from their native language.
  • The method is based on active repetition of vocabulary, notable expressions, and English sentence structures.
  • The app is especially suited for those currently at A1 or A2 level who want to build a solid vocabulary step by step.
  • Studying consistently every day, even just 20 minutes, yields concrete results over the medium to long term.

The Main Screen

  • At the top you'll find your session summary: minutes studied today and number of cards completed.
  • The progress ring shows the percentage reached relative to your daily goal.
  • Below the header you'll find filters to customize your study session.
  • Use the "Study" button to immediately start the session with the selected settings.

Study Filters

  • SOURCE — choose between system cards, your personal cards, or both.
  • LEVELS — select one or more CEFR levels (A1 → C2) to focus on.
  • CATEGORIES — filter by topic (e.g. Work, Travel, Sports…). No selection = all categories.
  • DIRECTION — study from Italian → English, English → Italian, or with audio listening.
  • CARD COUNT — how many cards you want to study per session (5, 10, or 20).

How to Study with Flashcards

  • Each card shows the word to translate first. Think of the answer before flipping the card.
  • Tap the card (or the "Show answer" button) to see the translation and example.
  • Use the review buttons to indicate when to see the card again: In 5 min, Tomorrow, 7 days, 1 month, 6 months, or 1 year.
  • Cards marked as difficult reappear sooner in subsequent sessions.
  • Tap the audio icon 🔊 to hear the English pronunciation.

The Spaced Repetition System

  • The app uses spaced repetition: cards you know well come back less often.
  • Each card has a review interval that increases each time you answer correctly.
  • Cards with an expired interval are prioritized in study sessions.
  • Over time you'll see the percentage of mastered cards improve in the progress chart.

Your Progress

  • "My Progress" shows how many cards you've seen, how many mastered, and the distribution by level.
  • "Recent Progress" lists the last cards studied with the rating received.
  • "Next Session" shows cards expiring soon that need review.
  • "Latest Cards Added" lists the most recently added cards to the catalog.

Audio & TTS Settings

  • From Settings you can enable automatic reading of the word or example.
  • You can separately enable Italian and English audio for the word and example.
  • Adjust reading speed: 0.8× (slow), 1× (normal), 1.2× or 1.3× (fast).
  • Set your daily goal in minutes: 20, 40, or 60 minutes.

Study Reset

  • The "Reset Study" option in the menu resets all progress: intervals, ratings, and statistics.
  • This action is irreversible and requires confirmation with your password.
  • Use it only if you want to start studying from scratch.
