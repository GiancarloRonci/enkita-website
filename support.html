Frequently Asked Questions

How does the repetition system work?

Cards you answered correctly are postponed, while difficult ones return more often. The goal is to make you review each word just as you're about to forget it.

Can I add custom words?

Yes. From the main screen you can access the "My Cards" section to add your personal vocabulary and study it alongside the system vocabulary.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account within the app. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days. This action is irreversible.

I forgot my password, how do I recover it?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a link to reset your password.

Is the app free?

Enkita offers free features for all users. Some advanced features will be available with the Enkita Pro subscription, coming soon.
